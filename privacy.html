Who we are

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information, including mobile numbers used for SMS.

Information we collect

  • Event and order details for catering, cafe, and rental services

Information collected automatically

  • Website usage data and cookies needed for site function/analytics

How we use your information

  • Process orders, bookings, and payments
  • Send order confirmations, booking updates, and respond to customer inquiries
  • Provide customer care by SMS when you opt in

Text messaging (SMS)

If you opt in to SMS — by texting our business number (949) 782-7468 (published on thepicniccollective.com and at our cafe), providing your mobile number in person, or during booking with consent — we may send:

  • Order confirmations and updates
  • Booking and appointment-related updates
  • Customer-care replies about menu, hours, catering, and services

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for help. Support: information@thepicniccollective.com or (949) 782-7468.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Mobile opt-in data and consent will not be sold or shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

How we share information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with:

  • Payment processors and service providers needed to operate our business, under confidentiality obligations

  • Legal authorities when required by law

These service providers are not authorized to use your mobile information for their own marketing.

Data security

We use industry-standard safeguards appropriate to our business, including secure payment processing through PCI-aware providers.

Your rights

  • California residents may have additional CCPA rights; contact us to exercise them
